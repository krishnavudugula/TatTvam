Council of Mentors

Consult the council of sages, warriors, and historical figures. Each offers a distinct philosophical and practical perspective on your dilemmas.

Epic Characters

कृ

Sri Krishna

The Charioteer

Guides Arjuna and seekers alike, offering cosmic perspectives on duty, action, and detachment.

अ

Arjuna

The Archer

Faces battles with deep self-inquiry, seeking to balance duty with compassion during high-stakes crises.

ह

Hanuman

The Devotee

Represents absolute strength through complete humility, overcoming obstacles through selfless devotion.

भी

Bhishma

The Patriarch

Stands steadfast by oaths, sacrificing personal joy for institutional duty and societal contract.

वि

Vidura

The Counselor

Teaches satya and vidura-niti - practical ethics, family unity, and simple righteous living for daily life.

चा

Chanakya

The Strategist

Applies sharp pragmatism, statecraft, economics, and strategic foresight to execute goals and out-think rivals.

Historical Mentors

वि

Swami Vivekananda

The Awakening Roar

Channels immense willpower, fearlessness, and the practical application of Vedanta to modern struggles.

शं

Adi Shankaracharya

The Non-Dualist

Cuts through illusion with razor-sharp logic. Teaches Advaita (non-duality) and supreme detachment.

शि

Shivaji Maharaj

The Dharmic King

Embodies righteous action, leadership, protecting dharma against all odds, and honoring all.

मी

Mirabai

The Divine Lover

Represents absolute surrender and blissful devotion. Teaches that love for the divine supersedes societal norms.

अ

Arjuna
