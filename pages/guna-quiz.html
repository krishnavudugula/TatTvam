Discover Your Guna

According to the Bhagavad Gita, nature binds the soul through three gunas - Sattva, Rajas, and Tamas. Understanding your dominant guna reveals your patterns of thought, energy, and behavior.

सत्त्वं रजस्तम इति गुणाः प्रकृतिसम्भवाः
Bhagavad Gita 14.5
☀️

Sattva

सत्त्व

The quality of light, purity, goodness, and harmony. When Sattva dominates, you experience clarity, peace, wisdom, and a natural orientation toward truth and selfless service.

🔥

Rajas

रजस्

The quality of energy, passion, ambition, and movement. When Rajas dominates, you feel driven, competitive, eager to achieve, but also prone to restlessness, desire, and anxiety.

🌑

Tamas

तमस्

The quality of inertia, stability, rest, and darkness. When Tamas dominates, you experience stillness, sleep, grounding, but also lazy procrastination, confusion, and resistance to change.
